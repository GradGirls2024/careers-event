    IFM Investors was established more than 25 years ago with the aim to invest, protect and grow the long-term retirement savings of working people. Owned by a group of Australian industry super funds, IFM Investors has approximately A$222.4 billion under management as at 31 March 2024. Operating from 13 offices globally – Melbourne, Sydney, London, Berlin, Zurich, Amsterdam, Milan, Warsaw, New York, Houston, Hong Kong, Seoul and Tokyo – IFM manages investments across infrastructure, debt, listed equities and private equity assets. For more information, visit www.ifminvestors.com.

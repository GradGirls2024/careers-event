Ask Me Anything with a Chemical Engineer/Science background expand_more
I have a who has worked in Manufacturing & Process Engineering, Project Management, Automation and Robotics, Environmental Management, Energy, Waste, Water and Groundwater optimisation and protection, HR: Training and Organisational development, IT – Management & Improvement of Enterprise Systems. Ideas on what to ask:
  • - Potential roles/industries for Engineering graduates
  • - Difference between learning the theories and applying them in the workplace
  • - Importance of mentoring and sponsorship on your careers
  • - Navigating male dominated industries
  • - Continuous Learning and Development – Having Growth Mindset
  • - Future skills
  • - Or anything really!
Ask Me Anything with a Development Engineer who develops accessories for our vehicles expand_more
This role involves project management with tight deadlines, product design and testing. I am passionate about our brand and have also been given the roll to work closely with Sales and Marketing to ensure our accessories are captured and promoted to their full potential.

Ideas on what to ask:
  • - What is it like being an Engineer at TMCA?
  • - What type of projects do you work on and what is involved?
  • - What type of people are TMCA looking for?
  • - Are there career development opportunities at TMCA?
  • - How do you balance work-life responsibilities?

Ask Me Anything with a Robotics Engineer expand_more
I have spent most of my career in the automotive and defence sectors. I have performed varied roles including designing shock absorber test rigs, calibrating engines, designing autonomous vehicles and leading a team of engineers and technicians. Currently I am the Multimedia Lead Engineer, responsible for ensuring the successful delivery of the next generation in vehicle infotainment. I have a passion for mentoring young engineers and encouraging women into the areas of STEM.

Ideas on what to ask:
  • - Why it is important to experience a wide range of roles/experiences within your career
  • - Why culture is important within a workplace
  • - What roles exist in the workplace for engineers (there may be some you haven't thought of)
  • - How being a technical boffin doesn't have to be your pathway as an engineer
  • - How being a technical boffin can be your pathway
  • - Why finding a mentor and a champion is important
  • - How to make gin
  • - Or anything else you can think of…

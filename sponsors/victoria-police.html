Victoria Police

Victoria Police is dedicated to ensuring the safety and well-being of the community we serve. We believe in fostering positive relationships with the diverse communities we serve, promoting inclusivity and understanding.

Our contributions to the community go beyond traditional policing. We strive to be proactive in preventing crime through education, awareness campaigns, and partnerships with community organisations. Victoria Police remains at the forefront of innovation in law enforcement and digital technology. We embrace technology and modern policing strategies to effectively respond to emerging challenges and changing community needs. If you observe our officers, they are decked out with Tech gear and we need ESTEAM students like you to design, develop, implement, report and support the tech used by our officers in order to keep them safe while they are on duty, so that they can serve our community and create a safer and stronger Victoria for all.

AMA - Government Recruitment Process and Technology in Victoria Police expand_more
The job application process to work in government is quite different to how the corporate world recruits. VPS is the process used to hire resources for all government agencies in Victoria. Come have a chat with us to find out the differences in government recruitment process and improve your chances of getting your application shortlisted. We are also discussing Technology in Policing today. Victoria Police uses technology in almost every aspect of policing. From intelligence gathering, to cyber security, to forensics science, we do it all! Come have a chat to find out how you can be involved in innovating new technology to further enhance and protect the lives of our community and front-line emergency services.

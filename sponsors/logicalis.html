Logicalis

Logicalis helps organisations achieve their digital dreams. They allow our clients like hospitals, universities, car manufacturers among many others know that we understand their pain points and have their technology needs in mind. Then we work with our incredible partners like Microsoft, CISCO, DELL, RedHat, Fortinet among many other technology players in the field - both established and emerging down to brand new founders to achieve these digital transformation for our customers. We help our customers with cloud and connectivity, cybersecurity, networking, modern workspace, employee experience and a myriad of other digital needs. Our vision is We are the partner of choice for customers, people, and the planet.

Today we are hosting some incredible team members from across Logicalis Australia who are here to answer your career questions. You will be meeting Cloud Consultants, Technology Managers, Business Analysts and Senior Engineers along with your questions about our sector and your CV.

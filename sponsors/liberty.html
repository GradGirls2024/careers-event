At Liberty, we champion diversity and proudly promote an inclusive culture with endless opportunities to learn, develop and innovate. We celebrate differences, so our lending solutions are customised to suit the needs of customers.

Our people are our most important asset – and we’re proud to offer an extensive range of initiatives to support diversity and inclusion within our community. We are committed to being a force for positive change and display that in our actions. Proudly B Corp certified, a Financial Inclusion Action Plan signatory and a WORK180 endorsed employer, we believe in making a difference beyond borrowing.

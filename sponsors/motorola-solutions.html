Motorola Solutions

At Motorola Solutions we are driven by our conviction that everyone should not only feel safe, but be safe. That’s why our mission is ‘Solving for Safer’ – we make technology and software to help protect people, property and places. Our radios help police officers, paramedics and firefighters communicate, our software lets 000 operators analyse and manage emergency calls, our smart video systems make cities and spaces safer in Australia and across the globe.

Today we’re running CV reviews, interview practice and Ask Me Anything sessions. We love what we do - come and ask us about it!

CV Review
Technical Interview expand_more
Ideas for questions:
  • - Why do you want to go into software engineering?
  • - What programming languages do you use?
  • - What software development tools do you use?
  • - How do you keep your skills sharp and up to date?
  • - Tell me about a time you overcame a problem in your software development?
  • - Can you tell me about a time you collaborated with a team to achieve a common goal?
  • - What are you working on right now?
Interview Practice expand_more
Ideas for questions:
  • - Tell me about a time you set a goal and achieved it?
  • - Tell me about a time you worked well on a team?
  • - Can you share a time when things did not go your way? How did you respond and what did you learn?
  • - Tell me about a time when you worked effectively under pressure?
  • - Tell me about a situation where you overcame a challenge?
  • - Can you tell me about a time when you made the wrong decision? How did you recover?
  • - Why do you want to work at Motorola?

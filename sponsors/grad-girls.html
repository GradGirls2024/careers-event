Ask Me Anything with Cheryl on Networking for Introverts expand_more
Drop in for a chat with Cheryl, our Grad Girls Program Lead, to discuss how to network when you’re a shy person or an introvert. Learn the steps you can take to figure out how to overcome the nerves and how to remember to breathe!

Ideas on what to ask:
  • - Is networking important for finding a job?

Ask Me Anything with a Professional Development Coach on the Importance of Wellbeing, Personal & Professional Development, and Self-Awareness for Women in Corporate Careers expand_more
Negin Shaygan is a Professional Development Coach from Sydney.

This AMA is open to Sponsors and Students.

In today's fast-paced corporate world, wellbeing is crucial for women to thrive and excel. Personal and professional development is essential for women in corporate settings because it empowers them to build confidence, enhance their skills, and achieve their career goals. It is also the foundation of effective leadership and professional success.

Personal and professional development allows women to understand their emotions, motivations, and behaviours, leading to better decision-making and relationship management.

Negin is here to discuss the significance of these aspects for women in corporate environments and how you can transform your professional and personal lives.

Feel free to ask Negin anything about personal development, self-awareness, wellbeing, or how these elements can benefit you or your organisation.
